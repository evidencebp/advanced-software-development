# Advanced Software Development

## Goal
The goal of the course is to help the students become ten times developers.The course considers various aspects of real world software development.
Each aspect is discussed and its importance is explained.
The students train directly on the aspect to evaluate their abilities.Later, improvement methods are given and impact is evaluated by comparing to previous results.

Students are encouraged to use AI in order to learn when it can improve their performance, and when it might be harmful.

See the [presentations](presentations).

## Requirements
The course is intended for 3-year/graduate students.Familiarity with coding is a must.
Experience in non-academic software development (e.g., open source, industry) allows familiarity with processes and concepts that will be used in the course yet are not part of it.

## Expected outcome
The students will be familiar with their ability for various development tasks: writing comprehensive and complete requirements
, identifying bugs, reducing complexity via refactoring, etc.
The students will be familiar with methods to train and improve in these tasks.
Advanced students can do a research project in the area.They will choose an aspect of software development
, justify its importance, provide metrics to measure it, methods to improve it, and training content.
